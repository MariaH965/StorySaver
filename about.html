First Contact

I'm so happy that you've decided to join me on this journey! My name is Mariam Hatmi and I'm a senior at GMU. I study data science, and this is my final project for ENGH375.


I've always been a big writer, and an even bigger reader. I'm on every major book or book adjacent website you can think of: Wattpad, A03, LiveJournal. Not just that, but I've always wanted to open my own blog but never really prioritized it. So I wanted to create something that blended my interest and desire together and came up with Story Savers . A combination blog, platform for stories, and a skeleton for what I hope to expand into an actual reading/writing platform with a community, not unlike Wattpad.


Let me know if you have any questions, complaints, or otherwise! Thanks for reviewing my project.
